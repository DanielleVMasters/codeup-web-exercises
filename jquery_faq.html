Are there trees?
Yes.
Are there animals?
Yes.
Can we camp there?
depends on the specific park
Are pets allowed?
Yes but must follow areas leash laws.
Can we bring food?
yes

Yellowstone

  • In Wyoming and Idaho
  • nearly 3,500-sq.-mile wilderness recreation area atop a volcanic hot spot
  • features dramatic canyons, alpine rivers, lush forests, hot springs and gushing geysers, including its most famous, Old Faithful.
  • It's also home to hundreds of animal species, including bears, wolves, bison, elk and antelope.
